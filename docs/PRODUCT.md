# Product notes

PulseLab supports two complementary moments: a researcher viewing aggregate study signals and a participant recording a low-friction daily check-in. The default demo study follows workplace stress over eight weeks.

The interface avoids clinical claims, clearly labels risk signals as follow-up prompts, and keeps participant identifiers pseudonymous. Production deployment should add authentication, consent/version records, encryption, audit logging, retention controls, and an approved escalation protocol.
